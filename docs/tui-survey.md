# SC-004 Operator Survey: TUI Detail Panel Readability

**Target**: ≥90% of surveyed operators report the new detail-panel spacing/colors make key fields easier to scan.  
**Response window**: 7 days after first deployment to operators.

## Distribution Plan

1. Share the survey link via the internal team channel after the TUI update is deployed.
2. Collect responses for 7 calendar days.
3. Summarize results in this document and in `docs/API_REFERENCE.md`.
